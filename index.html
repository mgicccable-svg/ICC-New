ICC Cable Corp.

Setting the global standard for world class wire and cable


Marine | Oil & Gas | Utilities | Building Construction

ICC Cable Corp. is a leading supplier of high-quality industrial wire, cable, and electrical accessories serving a diverse range of industries throughout North America and beyond. We specialize in providing reliable cable solutions for the oil and gas, marine, utility, and commercial building industries. Our commitment to customer service, technical expertise, and dependable product availability has made us a trusted partner for contractors, businesses, engineering firms, distributors, and end users.

Founded on the principles of quality, reliability, and value, ICC Cable Corp. maintains a comprehensive inventory of specialty cables and electrical products designed to perform in the most demanding industrial and commercial environments. Strategically stocked and distributed from our Houston, Texas warehouse, we are able to provide rapid order fulfillment, competitive pricing, and responsive support to meet the time-sensitive requirements of our customers' projects and operations.

Quality and compliance are the foundation of everything we do. ICC Cable Corp. is dedicated to supplying products that not only meet but often exceed industry expectations for performance, safety, durability, and environmental responsibility. We work closely with leading manufacturers whose products are engineered and tested to comply with the strict standards required across various industries and applications. Depending on the specific cable type and end-use environment, our products conform to widely recognized standards and certifications, including UL, CSA, IEEE, ICEA, NEC, RoHS, ABS, and U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) requirements.
